Legend of the Wraiths

Forged in the shadows of steep climbs and questionable life choices, the Chainring Wraiths ride when others rest. Not all who wander are lost — some are simply off-route without cell service.

We relish tailwinds, respect headwinds, and fear only bonks. Coffee is our elixir, snacks our mithril, and the drop bar our weapon of choice.
